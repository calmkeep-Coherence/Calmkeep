Calmkeep

Runtime Containment for Stable Generative AI

Overview

Calmkeep is a model-agnostic middleware framework designed to stabilize generative AI outputs during runtime. The system monitors symbolic rhythm, pacing, and contextual continuity across token generation and applies bounded-coherence containment to reduce contradiction drift and improve long-context reliability.

Technical Concept

Calmkeep operates between user prompts and model output, providing real-time containment and coherence stabilization without modifying model weights or requiring retraining. The framework is designed to function across multiple AI architectures through API-level orchestration.

Infrastructure Philosophy

Rather than correcting generative instability after output is produced, Calmkeep introduces runtime containment principles that maintain bounded expressive variation while preserving reasoning continuity. This shifts reliability from post-processing to active generative stabilization.

Research Visibility

Calmkeep is currently being developed as a runtime reliability infrastructure for generative AI systems. Research visibility, technical discussion, and evaluation inquiries are welcome.
